OUR
PARKS
01
POTTERNE PARK
Verwood, Dorset
A full outdoor skatepark in the heart of Verwood. Improvements coming in 2026 thanks to Dorset Council. Perfect for all abilities — from first-timers to seasoned riders. Spares & Repairs room on site stocking Blunt Envy gear.

02
RINGWOOD
Ringwood, Hampshire
Youth Riders' Ringwood base. Home to the annual Ringwood Skate Comp, open sessions and regular community events. Located at 39 Wessex Estate. Welcoming riders of all styles and abilities.
